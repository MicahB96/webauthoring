    <h1>French Toast</h1>
    <h3>by Micah Boyer</h3>
    <p>I will make french toast when it is a cold morning and I need something fulfilling to start my day. French Toast has been my favorite breakfast item since I was a kid.</p>
    <h5>Ingredients</h5>
    <ul>
      <li>4 Slices of Bread</li>
      <li>1 Egg</li>
      <li>1 Tsp. of Vanilla Extract</li>
      <li>1/2 Tsp. of Ground Cinnamon</li>
      <li>1/4 Cup of Milk</li>
    </ul>
    <h5>What to Do</h5>
    <ol>
      <li>Beat egg, vanilla extract, and ground cinnamon in small bowl. Then stirin milk.</li>
      <li>Dip bread in mixture, coating both sides evenly.</li>
      <li>Cook bread slices on lightly greased, nonstick griddle until browned on both sides.</li>
    </ol>
